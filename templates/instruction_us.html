  • The system doesn't apply to intra-day trading. Normally, the positions are held more than 1 day only if the stop loss or take profit price has been triggered.
  • Assumptions:
    • The system process the historical data, and predict the trading plan for next day: buy/sell/no operation, limit order, take profit and stop loss.
    • If the predicted signal is 1, hold the position if you have long position, close the position and then buy if have short position, buy if no position.
    • If the predicted signal is -1, hold the position if you have short position, close the position and then short if have long position, sell if no position.
    • The order is placed with the predicted limit price, take profit and stop loss before the market opens. Assume no slippage and no market impact.
